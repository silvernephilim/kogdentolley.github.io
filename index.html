Who are we? Silver Nephilim Crafts was established by a small group of friends with a natural love for creating unique art pieces. We offer a wide range of inventory including ear rings, pendants, paintings, wood burnings, photography, clothing and more! Our inventory changes frequently, and since the art is created by various individuals, the style and theme may vary.
How does the ordering process work? The items shown on the Jewelry and Artwork pages are available for sale and ship within 24 hours upon receiving payment. Custom orders require additional time prior to shipping, as we want to ensure you receive a quality product. Most custom orders are created and shipped within 5-7 days of payment. To place an order or request a consultation for discussing a custom designed piece, you can email us or fill out the form located on our Contact page.
What are our shipping and return policies? Our items are primarily shipped via USPS Ground. Per your request, Priority Mail may also be used. The listed price of a product does not include shipping costs. For more information, see the USPS Website. If you are not satisfied with your order, or your product is damaged upon receipt, contact us so that you may return the item and receive a replacement at no cost to you.
